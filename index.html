    • Hi, I'm Biely

    A passionate web developer exploring the world of front end and back-end development. I want to build a responsive and user-friendly websites that bring ideas to life. Whether it's crafting sleek designs, optimizing performance, or developing complex functionalities, my goal is to deliver solutions that exceed expectations. Let's create something amazing together!

    My journey as a tech enthusiast began in grade school when my parents bought our first desktop computer. It was equipped with a Pentium series processor and a bulky CRT monitor. I was captivated by the complexity of the CPU's circuitry and the transformative impact the computer had on our lives, sparking my curiosity in technology.

    Here's where I started...

    Driven by my curiosity for technology, I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering, a five-year program that gave me a strong foundation in physics, mathematics, electronics (both design and communication), and a basic understanding of programming.

    After graduation, I took on various roles that eventually led me to become a Senior Technical Engineer at FiberHome Technology. This role allowed me to work on troubleshooting complex systems, and collaborating with industry professionals, further honing my technical and problem-solving skills.

    Through almost four years of work experience, I developed a diverse skill set. My passion for learning and growth have led me to enrolling part-time in the Computer Systems Certificate (CSC) program while working full-time as a Technical Support Analyst.
